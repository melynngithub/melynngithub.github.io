Multimedia 1

Develop your skills in multimedia and video production. Topics include how to film and edit video with special effects, manipulate digital photographs and compose digital art, draw and animate 2D scenes and graphic design, and design a website that will serve as your portfolio for the class. You will also learn how to use industry leading tools in the Adobe Suite such as Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and After Effects.
